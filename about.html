My Journey

I have come to love programming in my polytechnic times in year 2010. The reason is probably because my mind think systematically thus Programming suits me a lot.
My very first programming language was python. It was kind of exciting to make a turtle move left and right just by using codes.
Another year later I learnt about java, html, and php.

In my last year of polytechnic year, I was doing a final year project to create a crawler on twitter account to gather tweets for analysis using python. Back then it was hell of a challenge, so many things I didn't know. It was a lot of proof of concept and learning. Although I received an 'A' for the project, I still felt that I could have done more had I have more knowledge.

After graduated from polytechnic. I work as a backend web developer and use cakephp framework for the first time. It was an enjoyable working moment with a group of nice people. I went for further studies to complete my degree. I learnt a lot about systems security and algorithm in this course.
I was very interested in mobile development, thus I look for such job and here I am doing mobile and web development.
